Build Different.

a letter to the new builders

From Andrei, Angelo, Lautaro, and Brando. 09 March 2026.

For fifty years, building something real required a team. Capital. Engineers. Designers. Marketers. Operations. Legal. HR. An office. A runway. A board. Permission.

That era is over.

We are living through the collapse of the barrier to building. Not a gradual decline. A collapse. What took a team of fifty now takes one person with taste and the will to ship. What took months now takes hours. What required funding now requires only focus.

The tools changed everything. AI doesn't just assist—it operates. It writes code while you sleep. It runs your marketing while you build. It handles the 80% so you can obsess over the 20% that matters.

I. The End of Permission

This is not about replacing humans. It's about freeing them. For the first time in history, having an idea is enough. The question is no longer "can I build this?"—it's "should I?"

That's a better question. That's a more human question.

The old model: you have an idea, you raise money, you hire people, you hope it works. The new model: you have an idea, you build it, you ship it, you learn. No pitch decks. No permission. No waiting.

The gatekeepers lost. The builders won.

II. The Solo Founder Era

1 in 3 startups last year was built solo. That number is going up.

Not because solo founders are better. Because the constraint that required teams disappeared. The people who understand this first will build the future. Everyone else will wonder what happened.

III. Taste as the New Moat

But leverage without taste is noise. When everyone can build, the question becomes: what's worth building?

This is where humans shine. AI can ship fast. AI can scale. AI can optimize. But AI can't tell you what matters. AI can't feel the gap in the market. AI can't know when something is done.

Taste is the new moat. Judgment is the new skill. Knowing what to kill is as important as knowing what to build.

IV. Abundant Creation

We believe in a future of abundant creation. Not a future where AI replaces builders—a future where AI multiplies them.

A million new businesses built by people who never could before. Problems solved because someone finally had the leverage to try. Ideas shipped because the cost of shipping collapsed.

This is human flourishing. This is what technology is for.

V. The Movement

But this future isn't automatic. It requires new tools. New patterns. New ways of working with AI that don't exist yet. Someone has to build them.

That's what we're doing.

Build Different is a movement for the new builders. The ones who see what's possible and refuse to wait. The ones who ship first and ask permission never. The ones who understand that one person with the right leverage can change everything.

We're not here to talk about the future. We're here to build it.

To kick off this movement, we're hosting a hackathon in Milan on March 21st—a room full of builders coming together to create what's missing for this future to happen. If that sounds like you, we'd love to have you. Apply here.
